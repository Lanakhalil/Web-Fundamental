Study with us




Undergraduate level: AQU enrols approximately 1700 students every year. The admission process is thorough, and students must meet high educational criteria, as well as complete a successful interview.

Postgraduate level: There are more than 45 postgraduate programs offered over several different departments. Admission is competitive, and varies for each faculty. Each program has an enrolment guide to refer to, or please contact the appropriate admissions team for more information.
